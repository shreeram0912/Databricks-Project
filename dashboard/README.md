* **Purpose:** This dashboard helps business stakeholders visualize restaurant sales performance over time, It highlights key metrics, trends, patterns, and comparisons at a glance.
  
* **Key Insights:**
  * Filter by Date Range (Start Date & End Date)
  * Allows users to view performance within specific periods such as:
    * Weekly
    * Monthly
    * Quarterly
    * Yearly

* **Core KPIs & Metrics**
  * KPI	What It Tells You
  * Total Orders	Total number of orders placed in the selected period
  * Total Revenue	Total money generated from orders
  * Active Customers	Number of customers who placed orders
  * AOV (Average Order Value)	Revenue / Total Orders
  * Unique Customers	Distinct count of customers ordering
  * Daily Sales	Volume of sales per day
  * Best Selling Items	Most ordered menu items
  * Order Volume by Day of Week	Identifies busiest days
  * Peak Hour Heatmap	Shows busiest hours by day
  * Revenue by Order Type	Compare delivery vs pickup
  * Revenue by Food Category	Which category drives revenue

* **Trends & Patterns**
    * Order Growth Over Time
    * Shows rising or falling order trends based on selected dates.
    * Example pattern:
      * Peak orders around weekends
      * Slow weekdays
    * Revenue Patterns
      * Compare revenue fluctuations: Seasonal peaks, Impact of promotions or offers
    * Customer Engagement
      * Track Active and Unique customers: Are repeat customers increasing?, Is customer churn decreasing?
    * Time-based Patterns
      * Peak Hour Analysis (Heatmap): Reveals hours when most orders are placed, Useful for staffing & kitchen readiness
      * Order Volume by Day of Week: Compare weekdays vs weekends, Identify high-demand days

* **Business Value**
  * Enables data-driven marketing timing
  * Helps optimize staff scheduling
  * Insights for menu optimization
  * Valuable input for revenue forecasting
